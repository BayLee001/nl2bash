NAME
       nm-tool - utility to report NetworkManager state and devices

SYNOPSIS
       nm-tool

DESCRIPTION
       The nm-tool utility provides information about NetworkManager, device, and wireless networks.

SEE ALSO
       NetworkManager(8), nm-applet(1), nm-connection-editor(1).
