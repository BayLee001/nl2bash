NAME
       intel_ - Polls the values of different reports about laptop lid state.

SYNOPSIS
       intel_lid

DESCRIPTION
       intel_lid  is a tool to poll ACPI and the BIOS scratch register's reporting of laptop lid state.  This can be
       used for debugging issues with laptop modesetting for lid opening and closing.
