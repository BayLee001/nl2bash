NAME
       docker-volume-prune - Remove all unused volumes



SYNOPSIS
       docker volume prune [OPTIONS]



DESCRIPTION
       Remove all unused volumes



OPTIONS
       -f, --force[=false]
           Do not prompt for confirmation
